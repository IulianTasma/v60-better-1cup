Adaptation notes
⚖️
Fixed ratio 1:16.7 – Multiply dose by 16.7 for total water. Bloom is always ~3.3× the dose. The 4 pours split the remainder equally.
🌡️
Water temperature: Freshly boiled water (~95°C) for all roasts. No need to wait after boiling, unlike the Ultimate recipe.
⏱️
Drain too slow (>3:30) → grind coarser. Drain too fast (<2:30) → grind finer. Do not change water amounts.
🔄
The final swirl (at 2:00) is essential – it levels the coffee bed and ensures even extraction to the end. Give it 2–3 gentle rotations.
☕
Difference from Ultimate: The larger bloom (3.3×) and repeated small pours work better with doses under 20g, where the coffee bed is too small for the 2 large pours in the original recipe.
